Watercolor Minis is about sharing watercolor art. Learn, Share, Appreciate the process.

This website is meant to share my painting process, and how I learned to get better over time in the medium of watercolor. In my blog posts, I share step-by-step instructions on improving your art, as well as my favorite materials and online resources. My favorite art to share is galaxy and space-related. My Paint Palette: Daniel Smith Watercolor Paints

I organize my colors in order of the rainbow (ROYGBIV). This way, I can easily choose my colors and know where each color is. My paints are mainly Daniel Smith Watercolor Paints and Windsor and Newton Watercolor Paints (Professional Series).

A Mini Watercolor Galaxy

Painted on a surface 4cm x 3cm in area, a galaxy with depth can be achieved. I used Daniel Smith Watercolor Paints, and Arches 100% cold press 140lb paper. Layering was my main technique.
